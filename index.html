Common Questions

Do I need to visit India personally?
For most processes like share recovery or banking documentation, physical presence is not required. We coordinate the paperwork remotely, though specific bank rules may vary.
Do you offer investment advice?
No. MVN FinHub is strictly a process and documentation support service. We do not sell financial products, offer trading tips, or provide investment advisory services.
How long does the IEPF claim process take?
Timelines vary based on government processing speeds and the complexity of the case. Generally, it takes between 6 to 12 months for final approval and transfer.
